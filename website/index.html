Lily

An open research laboratory for intelligence that remains close to human life.

Thesis

Open weight models are making inference portable. Lily studies how useful intelligence can run, split, route, remember, and be verified across the machines people already own.

The aim is not to replace the data center. It is to understand which work belongs on a laptop, a home machine, a small server, or an edge device, and when a larger model is genuinely necessary.

Why it matters

Intelligence should strengthen human agency rather than quietly remove it. Locality can preserve privacy, reduce dependence on distant systems, and give people greater control over the tools that shape their thinking.

Lily treats quality, safety, cost, energy, and human well being as parts of the same research question. Claims are measured. Limitations remain visible. Negative results are part of the record.
